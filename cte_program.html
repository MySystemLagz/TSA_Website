What is Blanson?

Blanson CTE High School is a brand-new school that opened late August and is a school built purely around the premise of preparing students that are ready to go into the workforce after graduation. Here, students are able to graduate with an industry certification, workforce certification, or hours towards an associate's degree.


CTE Programs Found at Blanson

At Blanson, Programs of Study are split up into three main academies.

Robotics Instrumentation and Process Control Distribution and Logistics Audio Video Production Animation Computer Programming
HVAC Construction Technology Welding Machining Collision Repair Diesel Technology
Hospitality Services Emergency Medical Technician Dental Assistant Physical Therapy Assitant Personal Care Technician

This is the Information and Engineering Technology (IET) Academy. Here you can find us animating, programming, building, and producing videos.

This is the Construction Manufacturing and Automotive Technology (CMAT) academy. Here you can find us building and repairing cars.

This is the Health and Human Services (HHS) academy. Here you can find us working in the dental, physical, and medical fields.
